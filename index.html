What We Do

From routine cleanings to complete smile makeovers, everything happens under one roof with doctors who already know your history.

Cleanings & Exams

Your twice-yearly visit is about more than clean teeth. We catch small issues before they become big ones, and you'll be in and out in under an hour.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Professional whitening, porcelain veneers, and smile makeovers. Dr. Blake and Dr. Hunter have transformed thousands of smiles over the years.

Dental Implants

Restore your ability to eat, speak, and feel confident. We use digital scanning so there are no uncomfortable molds involved.

Crowns & Bridges

Built to look and feel completely natural. Whether protecting a damaged tooth or filling a gap, the result is seamless and long-lasting.

Emergency Same-Day

A cracked tooth or sudden pain shouldn't mean waiting weeks. We keep time reserved for same-day emergencies. Call us and we'll get you in.

Sedation Dentistry

Nitrous oxide sedation makes any procedure comfortable. If dental anxiety has kept you away, this changes everything.

Meet Your Dentists

Two Generations of Care
Under One Roof

One goal: make your dental visits something you actually don't dread.

Dr. Blake Weber, DDS

University of Tennessee College of Dentistry

Dr. Blake has spent more than 30 years building a practice centered on people. His focus on cosmetic and restorative dentistry means he has helped thousands of patients in the Central Park and Aurora area get back smiles they feel proud of. Patients describe him as communicative, gentle, and the kind of dentist who actually listens.

Dr. Hunter Weber, DDS

University of Tennessee, Memphis

Dr. Hunter grew up watching his father treat patients with patience and care, and decided early on that dentistry was his path too. He specializes in restorative dentistry, Invisalign, and full mouth reconstruction. He has a gift for connecting with patients who come in nervous, and walks you through every step before anything begins.
