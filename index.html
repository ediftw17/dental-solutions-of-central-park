Meet Dr. Blake & Dr. Hunter Weber

Two generations of care under one roof. One goal: make your dental visits something you actually don't dread.

Dr. Blake Weber, DDS

Doctor of Dental Surgery, University of Tennessee

Dr. Blake Weber has spent more than 30 years building a practice centered on one thing: people. He earned his DDS from the University of Tennessee College of Dentistry and his BS in Zoology from the University of Arkansas, but what patients remember is how he makes them feel at ease before any instrument is picked up. Dr. Blake's focus on cosmetic and restorative dentistry means he has helped thousands of patients in the Central Park and Aurora area get back smiles they feel proud of. Patients describe him as communicative, gentle, and the kind of dentist who actually listens.

Dr. Hunter Weber, DDS

Doctor of Dental Surgery, University of Tennessee Memphis

Dr. Hunter Weber grew up watching his father treat patients with patience and care, and decided early on that dentistry was his path too. He earned his DDS from the University of Tennessee in Memphis and his BS in Biology from the University of Alabama at Birmingham. Dr. Hunter specializes in restorative dentistry, Invisalign clear aligner therapy, and full mouth reconstruction. He has a particular gift for connecting with patients who come in nervous, and a reputation for walking you through every step of a procedure before it begins.
